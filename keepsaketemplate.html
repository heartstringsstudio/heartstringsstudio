For Susan — about her mother, Lenay.

The song

The story behind it

They didn't want the eulogy version of her — they wanted the Tuesday-morning one.

So that's where I started: not at the funeral, but at a kitchen table, with the radio on and somebody dancing who had no business dancing that early. The whole song ended up hanging on one thing Susan told me, and I built every line back toward it. I wasn't trying to make anybody cry. I just wanted them to be able to hear her again.
