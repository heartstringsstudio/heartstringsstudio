Common Questions

Everything you want to know

How does it work?
Fill out the intake form with your story. Tim writes a fully produced, custom song around your memories — heartfelt, one-of-a-kind, and ready to share forever. After submitting, you'll be contacted within 24 hours by email with next steps.
How fast will I get my song?
Most songs are delivered within 48–72 hours. Rush delivery is available for an additional $50 — your song is guaranteed within 24 hours, provided there is prompt communication between you and Tim throughout the process.
Can I pick the style or mood?
Absolutely. You choose the genre and vibe — romantic, funny, emotional, nostalgic, solemn — whatever fits your story best.
Can I approve the lyrics?
Yes. You'll receive the lyrics first with one round of revisions before production begins.
Can I give this as a gift?
Absolutely — a custom song makes one of the most personal and memorable gifts you can give. Many clients order songs as wedding gifts, birthday surprises, anniversary presents, or to honor someone they love. You'll receive all the files to share however you choose — privately or in front of a room full of people.
How do I share my song once it's delivered?
You'll receive a private YouTube link along with your MP3 and MP4 files. The YouTube link is easy to share via text, email, or social media — anyone you send it to can watch and listen instantly. You can also download the files and play them at an event, post them publicly, or keep them just for family.
Is my song really one-of-a-kind?
Always. Every song is built from scratch — no templates, no shortcuts. Your story is yours alone.
